Our Story

Brian and Hong met through mutual friends in 2008 while attending the University of California, Davis. Hong would occasionally drop by one of Brianâ€™s many raging house parties and they maintained a very casual friendship. Unbeknownst to Hong, Brian always thought there was chemistry whenever they would hang out but the timing was never right. After graduating, Hong moved to Sacramento and Brian moved back to the Bay Area. While they both lived their separate lives, they would occasionally keep in touch through Facebook for the next few years. One day, sources informed Brian that it was time to make a move. It was right at that moment when Brian emerged from the shadows. What started as an innocuous Happy Birthday message to Hong soon turned into a flurry of back and forth texts, eventually leading to a first date.

After a successful first date, Brian realized Hong was even more amazing than he remembered and Hong realized that Brian was unlike any other man she had known. At the time, Hong was still living in Sacramento and Brian was still in the Bay Area. Despite the many miles and hours spent traveling to see each other, even if just for a day, they both knew that this relationship was worth the effort.

Exactly 3 years into the relationship and after many trips and adventures together, Brian decided that it was time to lock it down. On the day of their 3rd year anniversary, Brian asked Hong to go on a hike to Alamere Falls. He knew that she would not suspect anything because it was nothing out of the ordinary for them to do. After hiking to the bottom of the beach where a beautiful waterfall crashed against the sand, Brian got down on one knee and asked Hong to marry him. She said yes.
